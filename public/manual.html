1. How to Login

Step 1: Open the software.

Step 2: Enter your Username (e.g., admin) and Password (e.g., pointofsale).

Step 3: Click the "Login" button.

2. How to Sell an Item

Step 1: Click on the Sales module (Shopping Cart icon).

Step 2: Click in the big box that says "Find or Scan Item".

Step 3: Scan the barcode with your scanner OR type the item name (e.g., "Shan Masala").

Step 4: The item will appear in the list. You can change the quantity if needed.

Step 5: Look at the Total amount in green.

Step 6: Enter the cash amount the customer gave you in the "Amount Tendered" box.

Step 7: Click Add Payment to finish the sale.

3. How to Add a New Item

Step 1: Click on the Items module (Tag icon).

Step 2: Click the New Item button at the top.

Step 3: Fill in the details:

  • Name: Name of the product (e.g., "Milk").
  • Category: Type of product (e.g., "Dairy").
  • Cost Price: How much you bought it for.
  • Selling Price: How much you sell it for.

Step 4: Click Submit to save.
